Standard Operating Procedure for Safe Soil Integration
01
Co-Mixing

Blend biochar with moist native topsoil or FYM (1:1 ratio) to increase weight.

02
Broadcasting

Distribute uniformly across fields directly after primary tillage cycles.

03
Incorporation

Incorporate deep into the active root zone (10-15 cm) using rotary tools.

04
RDF Pairing

Apply recommended inorganic fertilizers to bypass early nutrient locking.

CRIDA Kiln Engineering Specs

The Central Research Institute for Dryland Agriculture (ICAR-CRIDA) portable carbonization kiln features several unique thermodynamic attributes:

  • Reactor Volume: 0.21 m³ (212 Liters) constructed from robust single-barrel carbon steel.
  • Draft Control Vents: 40 precision-cut openings (4cm diameter) staggered in 3 concentric circles to ensure uniform vertical updrafts.
  • Internal Chimney Core: Creates a continuous internal chimney channel using a temporary center pole, preventing core stagnation.
  • Anoxic Pyrolysis: Strictly limits internal oxygen feed, preserving at least 50% of raw carbon skeletons from escaping into the atmosphere.
